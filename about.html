About

We help travelers find and book hotels in Dallas, TX at the best prices, fast.

What we do

This site is a city-focused hotel directory and price-comparison tool. Browse properties in Dallas, see star ratings, amenities, and price ranges at a glance, then click through to compare live prices and book directly with the hotel or booking platform of your choice. No accounts, no fees, no markups.

Our brand partners

We list hotels from across the major brand families — Hilton, Marriott, IHG, Hyatt, Choice, Wyndham, Best Western, and independents — and link out to their official booking channels and to leading booking platforms (Hotels.com, Expedia, Priceline, Booking.com).

Affiliate disclosure

Most links on this site direct you to third-party booking platforms through affiliate programs. If you book through one of those links, we may earn a referral commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we keep the site free, ad-light, and free of pop-ups. Affiliate relationships do not affect which properties we list or how they're ranked.
