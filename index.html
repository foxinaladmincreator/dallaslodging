Dallas Hotels — Common Questions

The things travelers ask most before booking in Dallas.

How far is DFW Airport from downtown Dallas?

DFW International is about 18 miles (25–35 minutes by car) from downtown Dallas; Dallas Love Field (DAL) is closer, around 7 miles and 15 minutes. If you have an early flight out of DFW, staying in Las Colinas or Irving puts you 10–15 minutes from the terminals.

What's the best area to stay for a Cowboys or Rangers game?

Both AT&T Stadium and Globe Life Field are in Arlington, between Dallas and Fort Worth — hotels in Arlington itself or along the I-30 corridor are closest. From downtown Dallas it's about a 25–30 minute drive on a non-event day; budget extra on game days.

Is Deep Ellum a good place to stay in Dallas?

Deep Ellum is great if you want walkable nightlife, live music, and bars right outside your door — it's lively and loud, especially on weekend nights. If you want quiet, Uptown or the Park Cities are a better fit while still being close to downtown.

Do I need a car in Dallas?

Mostly, yes. Dallas is spread out and the DART light rail covers downtown, Uptown, and the airports but not everywhere. Uptown and downtown are walkable internally; beyond that, plan on driving or rideshare.

When is the cheapest time to book a hotel in Dallas?

Rates in Dallas are usually lowest about three to four weeks out, and mid-week check-ins (Tuesday and Wednesday) tend to beat weekends. Prices spike during major conventions and events, so check the city's event calendar before locking in a date.

Which area of Dallas should I stay in?

It depends on your trip — Downtown Dallas, Uptown, Deep Ellum are among the most popular places to base yourself. See our neighborhood guide for what each area is known for and where to stay in each.

Do Dallas hotels charge resort fees or parking fees?

Some do. Resort fees ($25–45/night) and parking ($20–60/night at city-center hotels) are often not included in the headline rate, so check the full price breakdown before booking. Many hotels waive parking for loyalty members.
