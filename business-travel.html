Best Hotels for Business Travel in Dallas

If you're in Dallas for work, where you stay depends on which corridor you're working in. This is the practical breakdown.

Dallas sprawls. A hotel that's 5 minutes from one office park is 45 minutes from another. The four districts below cover where most business travel demand sits — pick the one closest to your meetings.

Downtown CBD

Banking, law firms, and consulting offices clustered around Pacific Avenue and Main Street. Walking distance to the convention center. DART light rail connects to the airport (DFW) and the Park Cities.

Anchors: Bank of America Plaza, Comerica Bank Tower, Renaissance Tower

Las Colinas / Irving

Suburban corporate corridor 15 minutes from DFW. ExxonMobil, McKesson, Caterpillar Financial. Hotels here are 30-40% cheaper than downtown for comparable quality.

Anchors: ExxonMobil HQ, Toyota Music Factory, Williams Square

See Las Colinas / Irving hotels

North Dallas / Galleria

I-635 / Dallas North Tollway corridor. JC Penney HQ (formerly), Galleria Mall, plus tech and consulting offices. 25 minutes north of downtown by car.

Anchors: Galleria Dallas, Addison Conference Centre

See North Dallas / Galleria hotels

Frisco / Plano (Legacy West)

Toyota North America HQ, JPMorgan, Liberty Mutual, Boeing. The newer corporate corridor — most growth has happened up here in the last decade. 30-45 min from downtown depending on traffic.

Anchors: Legacy West, The Star (Cowboys HQ), Toyota Plaza

Booking tips for business travelers

  • Book direct with brand programs if you're loyalty-eligible — Hilton Honors, Marriott Bonvoy, IHG One Rewards. The point earnings + status credit usually beats any 3rd-party discount.
  • Cancellation flexibility matters more than rate — meeting schedules shift. Filter for free cancellation when comparing.
  • Watch for resort fees and parking charges. Some hotels in business districts add $25–45/night in mandatory fees that aren't in the headline rate.
  • Wi-Fi quality varies wildly — premium-tier rooms often include faster Wi-Fi as a built-in amenity. Worth $10–20/night if you're on calls.
